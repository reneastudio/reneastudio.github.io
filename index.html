FAQs

Frequently asked questions

01.
How long does it take to create my website?
The usual timeframe falls within the range of 3 to 5 weeks, depending on the completeness of the data you supply (such as text/copywriting, images, etc.). The sooner we gather the data, the faster your website can be launched.
02.
What about the payment?
We split the payment into two installments. You'll make an initial payment of 50% after reviewing the website design mock-up and giving the green light for the coding or development phase. The remaining 50% is scheduled for payment 14 days following the first installment.
03.
Can I pay the remaining payment when the website is about to be launched?
I apologize, but we cannot accommodate that request. In our experience, website development can occasionally take longer than expected, as we may still need to wait for you to prepare and provide us with descriptions, images, videos, and other materials. That's why, after the initial payment, we will continue to send invoices for payment every 14 days.
04.
What are the requirements for me to prepare in order to build my website?
Depending on the website's requirements, but usually, we need:
 
  • Company logo or branding identity
  • Address and contact information such as phone or email
  • Company description (profile, mission, vision, history, etc.)
  • Product or service descriptions and advantages
  • Product or service descriptions and advantages
  • Company location on Google Maps
  • Business legality documents (if using a .CO.ID domain - Indonesia only)
05.
Do I need to purchase a domain and hosting on my own beforehand?
You don't have to. If you choose to have us create your website, you'll receive a complimentary domain and hosting. We'll take care of the domain and hosting registration. Additionally, we'll notify you about the renewal invoices for the domain and hosting in the upcoming years.
06.

07.
Can I update or make modifications to my website by myself?"
Certainly. We utilize WordPress, making it convenient for you to independently edit website content without any additional charges. Once the website is live, we will provide you with a guide on how to edit website content in PDF format.

Some of our clients take 2-3 months to achieve the #1 ranking on Google. However, there's no guarantee that your website will also attain the #1 ranking for the targeted keywords. Some of your competitors may have substantial marketing budgets or have been established in the field for years. Nevertheless, we ensure that your website is SEO-friendly right from the start.
