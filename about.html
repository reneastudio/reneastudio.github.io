Learn more about Renea Studio

A creative design agency offering web design, motion graphics, and branding services. Discover our passion for creating great things.

Started in 2020, from an offline creative design studio, Renea Studio was founded in Lumajang, East Java, Indonesia. Renea Studio offers a range of services, including web design, motion graphics, and brand identity / logo / branding. In 2024, Renea Studio expanded its operations to Melbourne, Australia, further solidifying our commitment to serving clients on a global scale.

Our Mission

At Renea Studio, our mission is to assist business owners in enhancing their productivity by streamlining the process of creating marketing content for digital platforms. We aim to provide comprehensive design solutions that allow businesses to focus on their core operations without being burdened by the complexities of digital content creation.

Why Choose Renea Studio?

With a focus on creativity, innovation, and customer satisfaction, Renea Studio stands out as a reliable partner for businesses seeking high-quality design services. We combines technical expertise with artistic vision to deliver tailored solutions that meet the unique needs and objectives of each client.

Our Services

At Renea Studio, we offer a wide range of services to meet the diverse needs of our clients:

  • Web Design: We create visually stunning and user-friendly websites that effectively communicate our clients' brand identity and message.
  • Motion Graphics: Our motion graphics services help bring static content to life, engaging audiences and conveying complex ideas in an engaging manner.
  • Brand Identity/Logo/Branding: We specialize in developing cohesive brand identities, including logos, color schemes, and visual elements, to establish a strong and memorable brand presence.
